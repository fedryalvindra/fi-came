OUR PRODUCT

FEATURES

Low-light

with a maximum aperture of f/2.8 at the wide end of the focal length range and f/5.6 at the telephoto end. This means it can perform well in low-light conditions and provide a shallow depth of field at the wider focal lengths.

Auto-focus

The advanced autofocus system in Fi-Came Lenses enables fast and accurate shooting, even of moving subjects. This is essential for capturing spontaneous moments with the precision and clarity they require.

Wide Aperture f/1.4

With this wide aperture, Fi-Came Lenses let in a lot of light, resulting in beautiful bokeh and soft backgrounds. This makes the main subject the main focus against a backdrop that creates an artistic feel.

ABOUT

Fi-Came is a startup company engaged in the world of innovative camera and lens technology. Founded with a passion for immersive and expressive photography experiences, Fi-Came is unwaveringly committed to revolutionizing the way we view, capture and share precious moments through cameras.

VISION

Fi-Came's main vision is to be a pioneer in the development of camera lenses that combine high visual quality, elegant design and advanced technology. We want to empower photographers of all backgrounds, from professionals to hobbyists, to create stunning and inspiring masterpieces

MISSION

Our mission at Fi-Came is to craft exceptional lens products that redefine clarity, precision, and creativity. We are committed to developing lenses that empower photographers, cinematographers, and visual artists to bring their visions to life with unparalleled detail and expression. Through continuous research, development, and collaboration, we aim to deliver lens solutions that elevate the art of capturing moments and storytelling.
